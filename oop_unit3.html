Analysis of the Lessons from This Unit:

This 3d unit of the OOP module was a significant leap into understanding the UML’s standardized notation for representing software designs. We focused on getting acquainted with UML’s syntax and exploring its models utilized at different stages of the software development lifecycle. One engaging practical activity involved creating a UML state machine diagram, for a washing machine.
Through Microsoft Visio I designed a diagram showcasing the operational states of a washing machine from being powered off to completing a spin cycle. This activity reinforced my understanding of state machine diagrams.
Overall, this week emphasized the significance of UML in object oriented design by demonstrating how it facilitates visualizing system structure and behaviour. However it also shed light on challenges associated with UML such as its complexity and the potential for overcomplication in simple projects. Nonetheless, this newfound knowledge will undoubtedly be benefitial, particularly as we approach the design document assignment due in Week 7.
